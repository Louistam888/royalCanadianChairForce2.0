About Us

Toronto's most iconic gaming chair retailer

about us

Since 1990, Royal Canadian Chair Force has been selling top-quality chairs for those working in tech.

Starting in the early 2000s, we expanded our product line into gaming chairs. We quickly found that there is a huge demand, and since 2005 we've switched over to selling gaming chairs exclusively.

We sell only the highest-quality gaming chairs to both professional and casual gamers. We are a factory-authorized dealer of numerous high-end gaming chair brands, including RK Racing, Republic of Gamers, Dragon War and more.

FAQs

AREN'T ALL GAMING CHAIRS THE SAME?

A common misconception is that any office chair will do for gaming. Scientific studies have proven this is completely false. For the duration of the time they are sitting, gamers need to alert, attentive and able to respond reflexively and quickly to rapidly changing stimuli. Gaming chairs are scientifically engineered to increase mental alertness, boost energy levels and heighten a gamer's senses during gameplay. Just sit in our of chairs and feel the difference for yourself!

IS THERE FREE DELIVERY?

Yes we do! We ship our chairs anywhere in the world, free of charge!

WHAT IS YOUR RETURN POLICY?

All our chairs come with a 30-day return period. However, this does not apply to chairs sold as clearance items, which are final sale only.

ARE WARRANTIES AVAILABLE?

We understand that buying a gaming chair is a huge investment. That is why in addition to the manufacturer's warranty, Royal Canadian Chair Force offers an additional store warrantyfor three years after the date of purchase for $250 CDN. Depending on the product you are purchasing, we provide either a product replacement plan or a tech repair plan. Visit our store or contact one of our knowledgable sales representatives for more information.

WHAT IF I NEED SPARE PARTS?

Sometimes screws get loose, springs get weak or go small parts go missing entirely. We provide our customers with any replacement parts for under $30 for free, provided they are still available and in stock. Contact one of our knowledgable sales representatives for more information.
